# QAF Clinics — Strategic Growth Memo

Dental Clinics | Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

---

## About

QAF Clinics (بيت قاف) is a dental clinic group located in Dhahrat Laban, Riyadh. This repository contains the strategic growth memo — a 36-slide presentation that diagnoses the current marketing inefficiencies and proposes a data-driven growth plan.

## The Problem

- 773 reviews on Tibbi platform with no structured acquisition strategy
- 80%+ of the ad budget goes to awareness channels (Instagram/Snapchat) with low purchase intent
- Zero organic visits from local Google search
- Only 1 patient per year from paid ads vs. 14 potential patients per month

## The Proposed Solution

| Allocation | Channel | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| 60% | Google Search & Maps | Capture high-intent patients |
| 25% | Retargeting (Instagram/Snapchat/X) | Convert warm leads within 5-7 km |
| 15% | Local awareness | Build long-term brand presence |

Monthly budget: 21,000 SAR

## Key Slides

- **Phase 0 (7 days):** Fix the foundation — Google Business Profile, call tracking, review pipeline
- **Short-term (30 days):** Shift 60% budget to Google Search, launch retargeting within 5-7 km
- **Mid-term (90 days):** Build referral engine and accumulated digital assets
- **Long-term (6-12 months):** Full local market dominance
- **WhatsApp Pipeline:** Automated lead nurture architecture
- **FAQ:** Addresses common concerns about strategy vs. tactics, channel selection, and failure scenarios
